Catfish Pepper Soup With White Rice

About Catfish Pepper soup With White Rice

Catfish pepper soup also known as point and kill is the most popular pepper soup in Nigeria. We also have cow tail pepper soup, Goat Meat Pepper Soup and several other recipes in Nigeria. I like Nigerian foods to where I find it difficult to cope in foreign environments. Well, I think this is justifiable; I have been making Nigerian foods right from my eleventh birthday. I think the first food I ever made was Nigeria Jellof Rice.

Why Point and Kill?
Well, catfish is the most popular fish in Nigeria partly because it can easily be domesticated and partly because it tastes nice in soups. In popular Nigerian bars, catfish are stocked in tanks or large bowls alive. As a customer, you can choose the exact one you want, then they kill and prepare it for you. So you point it out, they kill and make pepper soup out of it. Hence, point and kill.

Ingredients and Measurements for Catfish Pepper soup With White Rice

You can cook Catfish pepper soup with white Rice using these ingredients; So here are the required ingredients, I used just a medium-sized catfish, so this is the exact size of the ingredients that I used. You can choose to increase or decrease depending on the number of persons you are looking to feed and the number/size of the catfish you are preparing.

Ingredients Measurements
1 medium-sized catfish 1.2KG
seasoning cube 1
granid crayfish Half cup
sliced onions Half cup
Scent leaves a handful
Rice 2 Cups
Fresh Pepper(Red)
Salt to taste
Lion pepper soup spices a Teaspoon
Utazi leaves very little

How to cook Catfish Pepper soup with White Rice

Catfish Pepper soup with White Rice step by step methods;
  1. Wash and parboil rice for 5minutes, down and sieve water.
    Boil fresh water with a little salt and cook rice till
    Rice is done... Next Catfish Pepper soup

  2. Cut and Wash Catfish thoroughly with salt and boiled water of 90 degree Celsius to remove slime..

  3. Prepare all the necessary ingredients;
    pound or graind the fresh red pepper and crayfish together.
    Also slice the utazi leaves, onions, scent leaves and set aside in bowls.

  4. Transfer the washed catfish into the cooking pot,
    I advised to use a small pot if the fish parts are very close to each other,
    this will allow the proper incorporation of the ingredients
    and help you make a very delicious point and kill pepper soup.

  5. Put on fire, place the cooking pot which contains the Catfish on the fire,
    add slice onions to the Catfish, add grainded fresh pepper, crayfish
    and seasoning cube, salt to taste.
    Add pepper soup spice and boil mixture for 7 minutes...

  6. Then add water, about 3 cups.
    Make sure the ingredients are not suspended at the top.
    Cook 4 minutes then add all the leaves(scent and Utazi)
    Cook 1 minute and is Done!

  7. serve Catfish Pepper soup with White Rice...
